Roadmap

We've put together a very rough roadmap for this project:

Short term

  • Launch the ListenBrainz alpha at the Future Music Forum in Barcelona on September 17, 2015.
  • Observe the first batch of users break our server. See how it fails and then fix it.
  • Get some rest after our marathon of starting this project.

Medium term

  • Add listen counts: Right now we can't show the number of listens a user has.
  • More data views: Show more data views that highlight artists, releases and other users.
  • Listen sharing: To share listens with others real time and to consume listen streams from others.
  • Data export: To export our data on a regular basis.
  • Data import: Import of old Last.fm archives.

Long term

We have not worked out many long term plans yet. Clearly we are on the road to developing open source music recommendation engines, but that is still a ways off in the future.

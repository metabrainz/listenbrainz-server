Reset Import Timestamp

Are you sure you want to reset your import timestamp?

Our last.fm importer keeps track of the listens you've imported and then only imports new listens added after previous imports. To do this, we keep track of the timestamp of the newest listen submitted in your last.fm import. By resetting this timestamp, you'll be able to import your entire last.fm data again. However, this will not create duplicates in your ListenBrainz listen history.

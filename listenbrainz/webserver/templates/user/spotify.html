Link Spotify Account

Spotify features

ListenBrainz integrates with Spotify to bring you two features:

  1. Record your listening history into ListenBrainz.

    We will record your listening history permanently and make it available for others to view and explore.

  2. Play music tracks from ListenBrainz pages.

    ListenBrainz has a number of music discovery features that require full length track playback. You will need to enable playback to enjoy these feaures.

We encourage users to choose both options for the best experience, but you may also choose only one option.

A note about permissions

In order to enable the feature to record your listens you will need to grant the permission to view your recent listens and your current listen.

In order to play tracks on the ListenBrainz pages you will need to grant the permission to play streams from your account. Oddly enough, Spotify also requires the permission to read your email address, your private information and your birthdate in order to play tracks. These permissions are required to determine if you are a premium user and can play full length tracks or will be limited to 30 second previews. However, ListenBrainz will never read these pieces of data. We promise! Please feel free to inspect our source code yourself!

You can revoke these permissions whenever you want by unlinking your Spotify account.

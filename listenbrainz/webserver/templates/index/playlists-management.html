Synchronizing Playlists

There are many ways to sync your own playlists to ListenBrainz:

Music players

  • Foobar2000, full-fledged audio player for Windows: Playlist-Manager-SMP

Synchronizing via third-party music services

Connect your ListenBrainz account and import/export your playlists to some third-party services.

  • Spotify

For advanced users

Developers are able to import/export and edit their playlists on Listenbrainz using the Listenbrainz API. Information on how to do this can be found in the API docs.

Note playlist importing requires track MBID identification for every item; exporting requires some kind of content resolution of tracks by track MBID, title and/or artist, following JSPF or XSPF specs.
